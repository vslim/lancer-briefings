# THE CARINIAN REPORT

## Off-shore Wharf Explosion — Five injured and one dead as emergency services rush to scene

*6.25am on 26 August 5013U*

Five people were taken to hospital after an explosion at a building site in Carina’s Tango-15 off-shore platform this morning.

Four are currently being treated on-site at Pyxis Major burns unit — three are critical and one is stable. The fifth is in a moderate condition in Vela Orbital Health Ward.

The incident was reported by multiple citizens to police at around 9.42pm. **Chief Auden Hoyt** told *The Carinian Report* that firefighters at the scene were able to secure the situation and administer first aid swiftly and effectively. Multiple ambulances and fire trucks were at the scene till the early hours of the morning.

The explosion is now undergoing an investigation by both **IPS-N** and local authorities; **Chief Hoyt** refused to make further comment. Carina General Worker’s Union has also opened an investigation into the incident.

It is believed a fuel tank exploded at the distribution site. Overhead drone footage reveals remnants of burnt clothing, destroyed goods, and shrapnel scattered out and around the explosion epicenter.

Head Security staff, [name unreleased], on Tango-15 also declined to comment on the incident. 

Parent company **IPS-N Technologies** confirmed in a statement that an incident had occurred at the off-shore platform.

**IPS-N** representative, Sunetra Marshall, said the corpro-state will be working with authorities closely as well as conducting an internal investigation to establish exactly what had happened and its immediate focus is on supporting the injured workers, their families, and the team on-site.

Marshall has said **IPS-N** will not comment further until the investigation into the explosion is completed. 

**IPS-N** incident investigators remain on-site. They estimate it could be days before the cause of the explosion is determined.

**Chief Hoyt** mentioned the suspected cause of an explosion of this magnitude would likely be a 2.0.c3 fuel tank and if it indeed was such, it is important to know that tanks of that size should not be used while being housed indoors nor near live persons.

Tango-15 Site Director, Viktor Petrenko, conferred with our reporters that no tanks of 2.0 grade were being utilized by **IPS-N**.

The Tango-15 off-shore platform was developed by **IPS-N Technologies** and the Carina Council's development arm, CPA, as part of the council’s *Ship Safe Initiative*, where IPS-N distribute "anti-piracy technology" to bases throughout the galaxy. It is still unclear the full extent of the casualties and damages caused by the explosion. Some eye-witnesses believe that there may still be undocumented individual.

This incident is on the tail end of several recent break-ins that have occurred across the platform over the last 5 months. While nothing was ever confirmed stolen from the breached locations, it is yet unclear if this latest incident is connected.
